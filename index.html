Bella's Adventures

Help Bella get to her final destination: THE BEACH 🏖

To get there, she'll have to cross a field full of bushes, dogs and some treats.

Come along!

This is what you need to know:

  • She'll need to have energy left (energy > 0) and score 40 points (score = 40) in order to get there.
  • Every time she leaves a bush or a doggie behind (not running into them) she adds +1 to her score.
  • She should avoid running into bushes: they rest -2 of her energy.
  • She should avoid running into doggies: they completely empty her energy, she can play with them later 😃
  • Bone to the rescue! They give her +1 energy point.

Use the up and down arrows in your keyboard to control Bella's path
