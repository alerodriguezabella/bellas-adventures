Bella's adventures

Help Bella get to her final destination: THE BEACH 🏖

To get there, she'll have to cross a field full of bushes, dogs and some treats.

Come along!

This is what you need to know:

  • She'll need to have energy left and score 20 points in order to get there.
  • Every time she passes a bush or a doggie she adds +1 to her score.
  • Avoid running into bushes: they rest -2 of your energy.
  • Avoid running into doggies: they completely empty you energy, you can play with them later.

Use the up and down arrows in your keyboard to control Bella's path
